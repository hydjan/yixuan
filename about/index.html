About

Yixuan is a blogger who loves to record life. Her blog is filled with bits and pieces of life, making readers feel as if they were in that moment. Yixuan’s articles always carry a strong “air,” whether it is the fresh air of nature or the scent of flowers and leaves in the air. In her blog, you can not only feel the warmth and tranquility of the natural world but also feel her love for life and her appreciation of life. Whether it is a stroll on a forest trail or the bustle of the city, Yixuan is able to capture the air to the fullest in her blog.
